Recluse (tm) 2017-

Recluse is a First-Person Shooter, set in the year 2140, during the reliance of robotics
to assist in lowering crime rates across the globe. You play as one of Vivia technology's 
most advanced androids, the ST540 (Special Tactics v540) alpha edition, or otherwise code 
named "Elise," for which your mission is to infiltrate a compound, along with police and fellow
task force androids, that is marked as a supposed criminal headquarters. Upon ambushing, and 
accomplishing your mission, you begin to realize slowly after discovering a mysterious piece of 
information that you were tasked to retrieve... who is the enemy you were tasked to 
infiltrate, and who exactly are your creators?
